Unmask'd — Research on Gendered Digital Harassment

Lead researcher for Unmask'd’s flagship study on gendered digital harassment in Pakistan. The study combined digital safety insight, behavioral analysis, and tech-policy thinking to understand how women experience online threats — and how tech can respond ethically.

Scope:
  • Interviews, case analyses, and field observations across schools and youth groups.
Methods:
  • Mixed-methods research — qualitative narratives + structured surveys (n=200+).
Tech & Analysis:

Python (data cleaning + trends), Sheets/Excel, visualization modules, internal incident-mapping using Firebase + MongoDB logs.

Outcomes & Impact:
  • Identified recurring threat archetypes and developed practical safety guidelines.
  • Informed Unmask'd chatbot design and contributed to workshops reaching 200+ girls across 10+ schools.
